# Thoth - AI Dream Archive

Welcome to **Thoth**, the definitive archive of the human subconscious. Powered by **dreambase**, Thoth is a mystical digital sanctuary designed to capture, analyze, and preserve the ephemeral imagery of your dreams for the future metaverse.

## 🌌 Core Philosophy

Dreams are the "atomic imagery" of our inner worlds. Thoth treats every dream as a precious fragment of a larger collective subconscious, using advanced AI to decode patterns that would otherwise dissolve into the void.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎙️ Capture & Synthesis
- **Whisper to the Subconscious**: High-fidelity voice recording designed for the immediate post-waking state.
- **AI Transcription & Analysis**: Powered by Gemini 3.1 Flash, providing instant transcription and deep psychological insights.
- **The Divine Oracle**: Every archived dream receives a mystical "Oracle Sentence"—a cryptic but profound reflection on your subconscious state.
- **Imagery Tagging**: Automatic extraction of atomic symbols and keywords to build your personal "Dream Atlas."

### ⏳ Loss Aversion Mechanisms (The Addiction Engine)
- **Memory Collapse Countdown**: A 3-minute high-stakes timer that triggers upon waking. Capture your dream before the imagery "dissolves" and becomes unrecoverable.
- **Ghost Prompts**: Leveraging the Zeigarnik effect, Thoth reminds you of unrecorded fragments from previous nights, prompting you to "recover the lost signal."

### ⌚ Watch Mode (Experimental)
- **Deep Sleep Monitoring**: A specialized interface for circular displays that monitors your sleep state and triggers the recording prompt exactly when you wake up.
- **Complication UI**: A refined, watch-face inspired countdown and status display.

### 🌍 The Collective Archive
- **Global Imagery Map**: Visualize the distribution of dreams across the globe in real-time.
- **Trending Atomic Symbols**: See which symbols and themes are currently resonating in the collective subconscious.

## 🏗️ Architecture

Thoth uses a modern Monorepo architecture to support multiple platforms while maintaining a single source of truth for core business logic.

### Platforms
- **Web**: React 19 with Vite
- **Mobile**: React Native
- **Windows**: Electron
- **WearOS**: Android Wear
- **Apple Watch**: WatchKit

### Core Services
- **AI Service**: Dream analysis and audio transcription using Google Gemini
- **Data Service**: Data storage and retrieval using Firebase Firestore
- **Auth Service**: User authentication using Firebase Auth
- **Storage Service**: File storage using Cloudflare R2 and Firebase Storage

## 🛠️ Getting Started

1. **Initiate Connection**: Sign in with your Google account to create your private, encrypted archive.
2. **Capture the Signal**: When you wake, tap the microphone. You have 180 seconds before the memory collapse.
3. **Consult the Oracle**: Review the AI-generated insights and your unique Divine Oracle sentence.
4. **Explore the Atlas**: Search your history to find recurring patterns and archetypes.
