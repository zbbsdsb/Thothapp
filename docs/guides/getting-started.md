# Getting Started with Thoth

Welcome to Thoth, your AI-powered dream archive powered by dreambase! Follow these steps to start capturing and exploring your dreams.

## Prerequisites

- A Google account for authentication
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- (Optional) A mobile device for the Android app

## Step 1: Sign In

1. Visit the Thoth web app or open the Android app
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Grant the necessary permissions

## Step 2: Capture Your First Dream

Thoth gives you two ways to record dreams: voice or text!

### Voice Entry (Default)
1. On the **Record** tab, you'll see a 3-minute "Memory Collapse" countdown — capture your dream before it dissolves!
2. Tap the large microphone button to start recording
3. Speak clearly about your dream
4. Tap the button again to stop recording
5. Wait for Thoth to transcribe and analyze your dream using AI

### Text Entry
1. On the **Record** tab, tap "Type Dream" to switch to text mode
2. Describe your dream in the text area
3. Tap "Archive" to save it — AI analysis will still be applied!

## Step 3: Explore Your Archive

1. Go to the **History** tab
2. Browse your recorded dreams in a beautiful grid layout
3. Use the search bar to find dreams by transcript or tags
4. Click on any dream to view more details
5. Delete dreams using the trash icon (hover over a dream card to see it)

## Step 4: Explore the Collective Subconscious

1. Go to the **Global** tab
2. Explore the interactive world map showing dream activity by country
3. Check out the dominant imagery tags trending globally
4. See stats like active dreamers and total archived dreams

## Step 5: Customize Your Settings

1. Go to the **Settings** tab
2. View your dreamer identity stats: total dreams, streak, member since
3. Add your own Minimax API key to remove public quota limits
4. Try the experimental Watch Mode for circular displays
