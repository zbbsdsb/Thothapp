# Managing Your Thoth Profile

Your Thoth profile keeps track of your dream archive history and preferences! Let's explore how it works.

## Your Dreamer Identity

Your profile includes several key stats that are automatically updated as you use Thoth:
- **Total Archived**: Number of dreams you've saved to your archive
- **Current Streak**: Consecutive days you've recorded dreams
- **Member Since**: Date you first signed up for Thoth

To view your profile stats:
1. Go to the **Settings** tab
2. Look for the "Dreamer Identity" section

## Quota Management

Thoth uses a daily quota system for AI analysis when using the public API key:
- Default public quota: 3 dreams per day
- You can check your remaining quota in two places:
  - The footer of any page (shows used/limit)
  - The "AI Synthesis" section in Settings

To remove quota limits, add your own Minimax API key in Settings!

## Maintaining Your Streak

Thoth rewards consistent dream journaling with a streak counter! Here's how it works:
- Record at least one dream per day to keep your streak alive
- If you miss a day, your streak resets to 0
- Your streak is automatically updated when you archive a new dream

## Signing Out

To sign out of Thoth:
1. Go to the **Settings** tab
2. Tap "Terminate Connection" at the bottom
3. Confirm the sign out
