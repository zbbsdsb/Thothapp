## 🎯 Enterprise Feature Planning

### 🔐 Core Enterprise Features
- **SSO & SAML Integration**: Single Sign-On with Azure AD, Okta, OneLogin, and custom SAML providers
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Granular permissions for admins, researchers, and end-users
- **Audit Logs**: Comprehensive logging of all user actions and data access for compliance
- **On-Premise / Private Cloud Deployment**: Full control over data hosting and infrastructure
- **Custom Branding**: White-labeling with company logos, colors, and domain names

### 📊 Research & Analytics Suite
- **Bulk Data Export**: Anonymized or identified dream data exports in CSV/JSON/Parquet formats
- **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**: Custom dashboards for trend analysis, sentiment tracking, and pattern recognition
- **API Access**: RESTful API with rate limiting, webhooks, and SDKs for Python/JavaScript
- **Research Collaborator Workspace**: Secure shared workspaces for research teams

### 🛡️ Compliance & Security
- **SOC 2 Type II Certification**: Security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy
- **GDPR / HIPAA Compliance**: Data residency, right to erasure, and HIPAA-compliant data handling
- **Data Encryption**: End-to-end encryption (E2EE) for all dream data
- **Penetration Testing**: Regular third-party security audits

### 👥 Team & Collaboration
- **Team Workspaces**: Shared dream archives for teams and research groups
- **Collaborative Annotation**: Tagging and commenting on dreams within workspaces
- **Custom AI Models**: Fine-tuned Gemini models for domain-specific dream analysis
- **API Rate Limits**: Customizable rate limits based on enterprise needs

## 🤝 Partnership Models

### 1. Research Partnerships
- **Academic Institutions**: Discounted or free access for university research teams
- **Data Sharing Agreements**: Collaborative research projects with anonymized data
- **Co-Publication Opportunities**: Joint research papers and conference presentations

### 2. Technology Partnerships
- **Cloud Providers**: AWS, GCP, Azure marketplace listings
- **AI Platforms**: Deep integration with Gemini, Claude, and other LLMs
- **Sleep Tech Companies**: Integration with sleep trackers (Fitbit, Oura, Apple Watch)

### 3. Reseller & White-Label Partnerships
- **VARs (Value-Added Resellers)**: Resell Thoth Enterprise with custom implementation services
- **White-Label Partners**: Rebrand and resell Thoth as part of your product suite
- **System Integrators**: Implement Thoth Enterprise within larger enterprise systems

### 4. Enterprise Sales
- **Direct Sales**: Dedicated account management for large enterprises
- **Annual Subscriptions**: Tiered pricing based on user count and features
- **Custom Contracts**: Negotiable terms for unique requirements
