# Thoth Features

Thoth is more than just a dream journal; it's a sophisticated psychological tool designed to capture the most ephemeral parts of your consciousness.

## 🎙️ Capture & Synthesis

### Whisper to the Subconscious
A high-fidelity voice recording interface designed for the immediate post-waking state. It supports both voice and manual text entry for maximum flexibility.

### The Divine Oracle
Every archived dream receives a unique "Oracle Sentence." This is a mystical, AI-generated reflection on your subconscious state, designed to provide a moment of profound insight and a sense of variable reward.

### Imagery Tagging
Automatic extraction of atomic symbols and keywords. These tags are used to build your personal "Dream Atlas" and contribute to the collective "Global Imagery Map."

## ⏳ Loss Aversion Mechanisms (The Addiction Engine)

### Memory Collapse Countdown
A 3-minute high-stakes timer that triggers upon waking. This mechanism leverages the psychological principle of loss aversion—the pain of losing a memory is a powerful motivator to record it immediately.

### Ghost Prompts
Based on the Zeigarnik effect, Thoth identifies days when you've missed recording a dream and provides a "Ghost Prompt" in your archive. This creates a sense of an "unfinished task," prompting you to recover the lost signal.

## ⌚ Watch Mode (WearOS)

### Standalone WearOS App
A dedicated WearOS companion app (`android/wear/`) that works without the phone app. The watch connects directly to Firebase and records audio with a single tap — optimized for the immediate post-waking state.

### Complication UI
A refined, watch-face inspired countdown and status display that fits perfectly on small, circular screens.

## 🌍 The Collective Archive

### Global Imagery Map
An interactive D3-powered visualization that maps the distribution of dreams across the globe in real-time. See where the world is dreaming and what themes are resonating.

### Trending Atomic Symbols
A real-time leaderboard of the most common symbols and themes currently appearing in the collective subconscious.
