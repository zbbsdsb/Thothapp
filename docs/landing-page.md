# Thoth: The Global Dream Archive

## Product Introduction

Thoth is a revolutionary real-time, collaborative dream archiving platform that transforms the way humanity records, analyzes, and visualizes our collective subconscious. More than just a dream journal, Thoth is a sophisticated psychological tool designed to capture the most ephemeral moments of consciousness before they fade away.

Available on Web, Android, and iOS, Thoth brings together cutting-edge AI, beautiful visualizations, and deep psychological insights to help you preserve your dreams while contributing to a global archive of human imagination.

## Feature Highlights

### L1: Core Capture Experience

**🎙️ Voice-to-Dream Transcription**
- Real-time, high-fidelity voice recording optimized for the post-waking state
- AI-powered transcription using Google Gemini API
- Support for both voice and manual text entry for maximum flexibility

**⏰ Memory Collapse Countdown**
- 3-minute high-stakes timer that triggers upon waking
- Leverages loss aversion to motivate immediate recording
- Turn the pain of forgetting into the joy of preserving

**👻 Ghost Prompts**
- Identifies days when you've missed recording dreams
- Creates a sense of unfinished task based on the Zeigarnik effect
- Helps you recover lost dream signals

### L2: Personal Archive & Insights

**🏛️ Personal Dream Vault**
- Secure cloud storage for your complete dream history
- Audio playback of your original recordings
- Organized timeline with search and filtering

**🔮 The Oracle Sentence**
- AI-generated mystical reflection on your subconscious state
- Unique insight for every archived dream
- Creates variable reward and profound moments of reflection

**🏷️ Automatic Imagery Tagging**
- AI extracts atomic symbols and keywords from your dreams
- Builds your personal "Dream Atlas" over time
- Tags contribute to the global collective analysis

### L3: Collective Visualization

**🌍 Global Dream Map**
- Interactive D3.js world map showing real-time dream activity
- Visualizes distribution of dreams by country
- Watch the collective subconscious pulse in real-time

**📊 Trending Symbols**
- Real-time leaderboard of most common dream themes
- Discover what the world is dreaming about right now
- See how your personal symbols connect to the global archive

**⌚ WearOS Integration**
- Standalone WearOS companion app
- Single-tap recording optimized for immediate post-waking use
- Beautiful watch-face inspired UI for circular screens

## Core Value

Thoth solves a fundamental human problem: the rapid decay of dream memories. By combining psychological principles with cutting-edge technology, Thoth:

- **Preserves the Ephemeral**: Capture dreams before they vanish forever
- **Provides Deep Insight**: AI analysis reveals patterns in your subconscious
- **Connects Humanity**: See how your dreams fit into the global collective
- **Makes Recording Fun**: Gamified mechanics keep you coming back
- **Works Everywhere**: Cross-platform support for Web, Android, iOS, and WearOS
