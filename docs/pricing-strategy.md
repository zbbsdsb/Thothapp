# Thoth Pricing Strategy

Thoth offers two plans to meet the needs of dream explorers everywhere: a free Community Edition for personal use and an Enterprise Edition for teams and organizations.

## Plans Overview

### 🌱 Community Edition (Free)
Perfect for individual dream explorers who want to archive and analyze their personal dreams.

### 🏢 Enterprise Edition
Designed for teams, researchers, and organizations that need advanced features, collaboration tools, and dedicated support.

## Pricing Details

### Community Edition
- **Price**: Free forever
- **Quota**: 3 AI-powered dream analyses per day (public API quota)
- **Upgrade Option**: Users can add their own personal API keys to remove daily limits

### Enterprise Edition
- **Price**: Custom pricing based on team size and requirements
- **Includes**:
  - Unlimited AI dream analyses
  - Team collaboration features
  - Advanced analytics and reporting
  - API access for integration
  - Priority support with 24-hour response time
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Custom branding options
  - SLA with 99.9% uptime guarantee

## Billing & Payment

- Enterprise Edition subscriptions are billed annually or monthly
- Payment methods: Credit card, bank transfer, or invoice
- All plans include automatic renewal unless canceled
- 30-day money-back guarantee for Enterprise Edition

## Upgrade Path

Community Edition users can upgrade to Enterprise Edition at any time by contacting our sales team. Upgrades are processed immediately, and all existing dream data is preserved.
