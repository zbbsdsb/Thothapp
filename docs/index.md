# Thoth

**AI-Powered Dream Journal**

Thoth helps you capture, understand, and explore your dreams with AI-powered analysis.

## Features

- 🎙️ **Voice Recording** - Capture dreams immediately upon waking
- 🤖 **AI Analysis** - Automatic transcription and tagging with Google Gemini
- 📖 **Dream Archive** - Personal dream database with search
- 🗺️ **Global Dream Map** - Explore collective dream patterns worldwide
- 🔒 **Private & Secure** - Google OAuth + encrypted storage
