# Thoth — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: May 9, 2026**

Thoth ("we", "our", or "us") is a dream journal application. This Privacy Policy explains how Thoth collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile and web applications (collectively, the "Service").

By using Thoth, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information**: When you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your Google account name and email address for authentication purposes.
- **Voice Recordings**: When you use the voice recording feature, audio files of your dream recordings are collected and stored. These recordings are associated with your authenticated account.
- **Dream Content**: Transcripts, tags, insights, and any text you enter into Thoth are stored in your personal account.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: We may collect basic usage metrics such as app opens, feature interactions, and session duration to improve the Service. This data is anonymized and aggregated where possible.
- **Device Information**: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostic and compatibility purposes.

We do **not** collect real-time location data, browsing history, financial information, health data, photos, videos, or device identifiers beyond what is required for core functionality.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Authenticate your identity and secure your account
- Store and process your dream recordings and transcripts
- Provide AI-powered transcription and tagging via Google Gemini AI
- Sync your data across your devices
- Send you in-app reminders (e.g., Memory Collapse countdown)
- Maintain and improve the Service

## 4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, which employs industry-standard encryption:

- **In transit**: Data is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS when transmitted between your device and our servers.
- **At rest**: Data stored in Firestore and Firebase Storage is encrypted at rest using Google's default AES-256 encryption.

Your dream data is accessible only by your authenticated Google account. We implement access controls and regularly review our security practices to protect your data.

---

## 5. Data Retention

You may delete your dream recordings and transcripts at any time through the app. Deleting a dream permanently removes the associated Firestore document and Firebase Storage file.

If you delete your Google account or revoke Thoth's access to your Google account, all associated data will be deleted from our systems within 30 days, unless we are required to retain it by law.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Thoth is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

By using Thoth, you consent to such transfer.
